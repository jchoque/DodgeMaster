Dodge Master

  • Player Experience Goals: Use reactions skills to be able to dodge balls.
  • Genre: Skills

  • Setting/Premise: Bob the derp bird was lost in the big city of Ney York. All of a sudden he finds himself in danger and is forced to make use of his dodging skills to stay alive!
